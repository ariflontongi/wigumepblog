Suge Knight's Ex-Wife, Sharitha Golden, Responds To Recent Allegations Of Her Killing Tupac

In case you missed it, Suge Knight made headlines a couple days ago when he brought up a new theory in the death of Tupac Shakur in a recent interview from a Tupac Assassination documentary, claiming Death Row staffer Reggie Wright Jr. and his ex-wife Sharitha Golden were allegedly behind the murder of Tupac, but were originally attempting to murder Suge instead.

Well according to Sharitha, all of that is one big lie that makes no sense. Sharitha told TMZ that she's been hearing about this allegation for years, and that it's been having an affect on her children in the process, one of whom is Suge's daughter.

Sharitha is apparently so angry about all these recent allegations, that a lawsuit could be on the way TMZ reports.

“I’m so damn sick of getting 100,000 calls about this. No way in hell I would’ve murdered 2Pac. For what reason?” she asks in the TMZ clip. “This ridiculous theory that I had to get half of Death Row […] I already had half of Death Row, America. When 2Pac died, what did Death Row become after that? Zero. Nothing. A downfall.”

For what it's worth, Suge's attorney also told TMZ that the info purported in the documentary is untrue. Check Sharitha’s statement for yourself (below).
