Aisha Bello Mustapha: NTA Broadcasting Legend Is Dead

Nigerians are mourning the loss of the legendary National Television Authority (NTA) broadcaster, Aisha Bello Mustapha, in a somber turn of events. The news of her passing, announced on Monday, December 11, has profoundly impacted the nation, with many expressing their grief and reminiscing about the indelible mark she made during her career.

Details surrounding the cause of Aisha Bello‘s death have not been disclosed. However, reports indicate that a funeral prayer will be held in her honor at the central mosque in Abuja at 1 pm on the same day.

Aisha Bello served as the General Manager of NTA Parliament and was a familiar face on the NTA Network News at 9 pm throughout the ’90s and early 20s. She shared the screen with another veteran broadcaster, Cyril Stober, who reportedly retired in 2019.

Netizens have taken to social media to share their memories and pay tribute to the late broadcaster. Users have fondly recalled how Aisha Bello played a significant role in their childhood, with some referring to her as a “legend.”
