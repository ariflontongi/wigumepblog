Who is Lea Kyle? Wiki, Biography &amp; Facts About Magician

Lea Kyle (born in 1996; Age: 25 years) is a famous French Magician, Quick-Change Artist, Social Media Influencer, Fashion Designer, Instagram Personality, and Entrepreneur from Bordeaux, France. Lea is famous in the country for her amazing and quick dress-changing skills. Apart from this, she is also a talented magician and performed various quick-changing acts at so many magic competitions and talent shows. 

As per the sources, she is also a professional fashion designer and completed long-term training at Haute couture. According to her official website, she stepped into the professional magic career 7 years ago with her magician partner Florian Sainvet.  

She is perfect in all types of magic but mostly performs Quick Change. As per the sources, she has participated in various shows including the International Festival of Magic of Marly, French Champion of Magic 2019, Villebarou international magic festival, Penn and Teller: Fool Us 2020, and others. 

Kyle came to the spotlight after grabbing the Golden Buzzer in the audition round of America’s Got Talent 2021.
