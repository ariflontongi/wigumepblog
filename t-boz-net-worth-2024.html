T-Boz Net Worth 2024

T-Boz’s unique contralto vocals and her group’s blend of hip-hop, R&B, and pop made them one of the best-selling girl groups of all time. This article delves into T-Boz’s net worth as of 2024, her sources of wealth, and her financial journey.

T-Boz’s Early Life and Career

T-Boz was born in Des Moines, Iowa, and showed an interest in music from a young age. She moved to Atlanta, Georgia, in her late teens to pursue a career in music. In Atlanta, she met Lisa “Left Eye” Lopes and Rozonda “Chilli” Thomas, and together they formed TLC. The group’s blend of R&B, hip-hop, and pop, combined with their socially conscious lyrics, made them a sensation in the 1990s.

T-Boz’s Success with TLC

TLC released their debut album, “Ooooooohhh… On the TLC Tip,” in 1992. The album was a commercial success, selling over six million copies worldwide. Their second album, “CrazySexyCool,” was even more successful, selling over 14 million copies worldwide and earning the group two Grammy Awards. T-Boz’s unique contralto vocals were a significant part of the group’s success.

T-Boz’s Solo Career

After the tragic death of Lisa “Left Eye” Lopes in 2002, T-Boz and Chilli continued to perform as a duo. T-Boz also embarked on a solo career, releasing her solo album, “A New Day,” in 2017. The album was well-received by critics and fans alike.

T-Boz’s Acting Career

In addition to her music career, T-Boz has also had a successful acting career. She has appeared in several films and television shows, including “Living Single,” “Belly,” and “Days of Our Lives.” Her acting roles have contributed to her overall net worth.

T-Boz’s Other Ventures

T-Boz has also ventured into other areas, such as writing and fashion. She released a book, “A Sick Life: TLC ‘n Me: Stories from On and Off the Stage,” in 2017. The book details her struggles with sickle cell disease and her journey in the music industry. She also launched a clothing line, “TLC,” in 2019.

T-Boz’s Financial Struggles

Despite her success, T-Boz has faced financial struggles. She filed for bankruptcy twice, in 2011 and 2013. However, she has managed to bounce back from these setbacks and rebuild her financial stability.

T-Boz’s Philanthropy

T-Boz is also known for her philanthropy. She has been involved in several charitable causes, including the Sickle Cell Disease Association of America and the Lisa Lopes Foundation. Her philanthropic efforts have earned her recognition and respect in the industry.

T-Boz’s Net Worth in 2024
