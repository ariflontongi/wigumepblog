Did Your Favorite Actor Get a 2014 Golden Globes Nomination? See the List!

Ours did! The 71st Annual Golden Globe Awards nominations were announced this morning by The Hollywood Foreign Press Association—along with the help of emcees Aziz Ansari, Zoe Saldana, and Olivia Wilde—at the Beverly Hilton, and given the list of nominees that include both film and television stretching across 25 categories, along with Tina Fey and Amy Poehler co-hosting for the second year in a row, this just might be the biggest Golden Globes yet! As for the top leading ladies who will without a doubt stun on the red carpet, Cate Blanchett, Sandra Bullock, and Amy Adams received nominations for their leading roles in film, while Lupita Nyong'o, Jennifer Lawrence, and Julia Roberts were given nods for their supporting roles. For the small screen, Kerry Washington, Julianna Margulies, Zooey Deschanel, and Lena Dunham all were nominated for best actress in a television series. Scroll down to see the list of nominees and be sure to catch Miss Golden Globe 2014, Sosie Bacon, hand out the gold statuettes to this year's biggest actors on Sunday, January 13th during the live awards ceremony on NBC.
