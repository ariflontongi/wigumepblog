Oprah and Taylor Swifts Quintessential Fall Pants Are on Mega Sale

As a fashion editor, I spend hours researching dozens of trends daily; everything from dumpling bags to large-buckle shoes falls in my wheelhouse. While it’s easy to become jaded by the abundance of styles, one look always manages to stand out: cargo pants.

The big, pocketed bottoms have risen to the top of fashion charts with celebrities such as Sofía Vergara, Oprah, Taylor Swift, and Emily Ratajkowski all donning the style. I get the hype; they’re comfortable, uncomplicated, and quite frankly, the quintessential fall pants. Better yet, they can be worn with nearly everything. Graphic tees? Yes. Slim heels? Absolutely. Bodysuits? Even better. But things just got more exciting because tons of cargo pants are currently discounted ahead of Amazon’s Prime Big Deal Days, starting at the low price of $22. 

5 Best Amazon Prime Big Deal Days Cargo Pants

Wonderwink Straight-Leg Cargo Pants

This Wonderwink pair is available in 21 colors, sizes XX small to 5X large, and petite and tall measurements. The straight-leg silhouette features a drawstring closure, belt loops, an ID bungee cord, vented sides, and an elastic waistband for added comfort. The stretchy pants also come with five functional pockets to store all of your belongings, while a high-rise back provides additional coverage. Wear these pants with sneakers and a baby tee for a statement or don them to work, whether you’re a nurse, veterinarian, or dentist.       

Mofiz Drawstring Cargo Pants

Make like Taylor Swift and consider tapered cargo pants from Mofiz. These drawstring bottoms come in five neutral hues, such as light green, khaki, and black. Look forward to lightweight fabric that’s moisture wicking, quick drying, and breathable. Six pockets can also be found throughout the bottoms for practically, while elastic cuffs make these pants ideal for hiking by keeping nature out. One shopper even calls Mofiz’s pants great “travel pants,” saying she plans to wear them on her “next overseas flight” since they’re “that comfy” and can “carry [her] passport and cards in the snap pockets.” 

Lepunuo Stretchy Cargo Pants

Don’t forget to add Lepuno’s bottoms to your cart, aka Amazon’s number one best-selling cargos. For $30, you can choose from 13 fun colorways such as purple, gray, orange, and more. Further details include a zipper closure, cotton construction, water-resistant fabric, an elastic waist, and a plethora of pockets. Reviewers are just as excited about this Y2K style, saying they’re the “perfect length” for sneakers and heels and fit the “thighs and booty.” If you’re curious to see just how good they are, you’re just going to have to snag one (or two) pairs for yourself.
