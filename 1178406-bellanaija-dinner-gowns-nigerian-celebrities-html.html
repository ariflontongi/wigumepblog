BellaNaija dinner gowns of Nigerian celebrities

What are dinner gowns in Nigeria? These are luxurious dresses Nigerian women wear for special occasions like parties or weddings. Such gowns are mostly long and extremely beautiful with lots of decorative elements like lace, embroidery, rhinestones, and many others. There are many styles of dinner gowns in Nigeria and most of them are really cool.
