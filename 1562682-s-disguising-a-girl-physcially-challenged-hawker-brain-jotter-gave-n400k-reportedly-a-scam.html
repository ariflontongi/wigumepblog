He Is a Man: Brain Jotter Reacts As Physically Challenged Hawker He Gave N400k Reportedly a Sc

  • Skit maker Brain Jotter recently helped a young, physically challenged girl hawking water in traffic
  • According to reports online, the girl is a boy who disguises to scam people and has been helped off the streets several times
  • Some netizens claimed the boy, Sultan, is well known in Osogbo, Osun state, where he hails from
  • In a chat with Legit.ng, Rene Ahmed, a philanthropist who also runs a foundation, Visitahospital shared her opinion about the physically challenged boy's situation

In a shocking turn of events, the young lady comedian Brain Jotter helped get back into her makeup business has been reported to be a scammer.

According to reports, the lady is a man who disguises himself to get money and help from people, only to go back to the streets.

The report claimed the young man's name was Sultan, and in 2022, a group of people came together, rented an apartment for him and got him tools to establish himself.

In the video Brain Jotter shared, the hawker, dressed as a woman, called "herself" Eniola and revealed on further probing by the skit maker that she had to start hawking because her makeup business wasn't doing well.

Brain Jotter gave her N400k on the spot to buy the things she needed.
