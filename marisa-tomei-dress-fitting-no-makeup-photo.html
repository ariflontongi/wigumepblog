Marisa Tomei Looks Radiant in Rare No-Makeup Snapshot: Photo

Marisa Tomei is one of those stars who you can’t help but get so excited when they post on social media, because you know it’s going to be a stunning snapshot. And her latest photo of herself may be one of our favorites yet!

On Nov 16, the Spider-Man: Homecoming star shared a rare snapshot of herself with no makeup on her Instagram with the caption, “Fittings in NYC @gabrielahearst.”

While the Oscar-winning actress keeps her makeup on the more natural dewy side, she wowed everyone with a pic of herself looking as radiant as can be without makeup.
