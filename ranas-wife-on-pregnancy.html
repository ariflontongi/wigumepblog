Rana's Wife On Pregnancy!

Article by Nanda Gopal Published on: 12:59 pm, 28 October 2022

Rumours were abuzz that Rana Daggubati’s wife Miheeka Bajaj is pregnant. Speculations were rife that the couple, who got married on Aug 8, 2020, were expecting their first child. The reports of Miheeka carrying went viral. Fans of Rana were thrilled with the reports. However, Miheeka herself responded to the pregnancy rumours. She clarified that she is not pregnant and said she is happily married but not pregnant. This is enough to bust the ongoing rumours.

Earlier, there were some rumours about the couple getting separated. By stating “Happily Married”, Miheeka has busted both the rumours at one go. Rana Daggubati was last seen opposite Pawan Kalyan in Bheemla Nayak.

His Virata Parvam received critical acclaim but failed to do well commercially at the Box Office. Rana has acted in a Netflix series Rana Naidu which has his Babayi Venkatesh Daggubati. The actor is listening to a lot of scripts and is contemplating signing some good films. More deets are awaited on the work front.
