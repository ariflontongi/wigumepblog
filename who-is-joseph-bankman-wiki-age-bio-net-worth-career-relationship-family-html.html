ReadyReturn Concept

In 2004, Joseph Bankman and his colleagues introduced the concept of “ReadyReturn” in California. This innovative application aimed to simplify tax filing for taxpayers by prefilling their tax forms, requiring only minimal corrections from the users. Unfortunately, the program faced lobbying opposition from tax preparation software manufacturer, Intuit, and was reportedly defeated by one vote in the California Assembly.
