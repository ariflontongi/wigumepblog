FAQs

Does Billie have free shipping?

Billie has free shipping for any orders over $15. If you have a razor or Wonder Wipes subscription, you can also get free shipping. Standard shipping for all other orders costs $4.  

How do I get a first order discount at Billie?

Billie does not have specific first-order discounts. However, we do have a regular 10% off discount for razors and sets on this Marie Claire page. You can also sign up for the Billie newsletter on their homepage. When you sign up, Billie will send you the latest sales and brand news.  

Does Billie have Black Friday sales?

Billie had its first Black Friday sale in 2020. Last year, Billie also had 20% off orders for Black Friday. We expect similar savings this year so check this page for the latest updates. You might be able to save on your razors, soap, and sets. 

How much are the subscription costs at Billie?

You can order subscriptions on a monthly basis or choose 2 or 3-month subscriptions. Billie shaving kits usually start from $10 per month, with a cost for the initial Starter Kit as well. Any subscription orders also get free shipping. 

What is the return policy at Billie?

Billie accepts 30-day returns if your order arrives damaged, not complete, or lost. Billie does not accept any returns on items other than this due to hygiene reasons.
