Who is Kirk Herbstreit?

Born on August 19, 1969, in Centerville, Ohio, Kirk Herbstreit is a sports analyst.

Currently, he works for ESPN, on the show, College GameDay, which discusses college football.

During his college days, he was a star football player for Ohio State University. To add to his credentials, he is a five-time Sports Emmy Award winner.
