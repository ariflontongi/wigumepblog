What is a micro cat?

What is a "micro" kitten? ​ The term 'micro' is used to describe the size of any kitten that will measure 8" to 10" at the shoulder and will weigh 4 to 7 lbs or less when fully grown.

    Can a cat be a dwarf?

    Dwarfism in cats is a condition caused by genetic anomalies which lead to stunted growth and abnormal feline body proportions. Unlike other variants of small cats, feline dwarfs are often associated with numerous health issues.

    How big is a miniature cat?

    Miniature Cats

    Unlike dwarf cats, miniatures have the same proportions as their regularly-sized cousins. Miniatures generally don't exceed 10 inches in height at the shoulder and 1 foot from neck to tail. At maturity, they shouldn't exceed 7 pounds.
