Best Phone Wallets for iPhone, Samsung, Google Devices 2024

If you purchase an independently reviewed product or service through a link on our website, The Hollywood Reporter may receive an affiliate commission.

Whether you’re heading to a concert or running around the city (or set), life is so much easier when you have less to keep track of. In addition to flashy cases and hands-free straps, another tech accessory we love is the phone wallet — an all-in-one holder that easily attaches to your phone.

Available in stick-on styles (compatible with most smartphone models) and magnetic designs (compatible only with MagSafe devices and cases), there are tons of phone wallets to choose from, depending on your preference.

A slim design is all you need if you’re low-maintenance and only travel with one debit or credit card and ID. If you never leave home without enough cash to tip valet, then an expandable style is the right choice. If you’re stressed that your cards might slip out, then a snap-closure wallet will put your mind at ease.

BEST FASHION WALLET

Sonix Magnetic Wallet

For a metallic moment, we’re here for Sonix’s magnetic wallet, featuring a metallic platinum sheen. Easily attachable to any MagSafe compatible device or case, this wallet holds up to four cards.

BEST CROSSBODY phone WALLET

Bandolier Emma Crossbody Phone Case and Wallet

Rounding out this list is Bandolier’s iconic crossbody phone case and wallet, available in three colorways and equipped with an adjustable strap for hands-free fun. Compatible with iPhone, this wallet features durable pebble leather that softens over time, and two snap closure pockets to securely carry your cards and cash.

BEST value

Puxnoin Phone Card Holder

Compatible with a range of models (iPhone, Android, Samsung Galaxy and more), this stick-on phone card holder is a steal for its staying power. Thanks to high-quality 3M adhesive tape, this wallet sticks to your phone and can easily be removed sans sticky residue. Available in a convenient two-pack.

BEST phone WALLET UNDER $10

M-Plateau Card Holder

If you’re looking for a more concealed style, M-Plateau’s stylish card holder features a snap button closure to keep your cards secure. Compatible with most smartphones, this wallet features a 3M adhesive to stick onto your desired phone case. Bonus: this wallet style makes for a great kickstand; just open the wallet and position your phone in portrait or landscape mode for hands-free photos and calls. Available in a variety of colors and prints.

Best Custom Wallet

Casetify Custom MagSafe Wallet

Prefer designs inspired by Disney, art and more? Casetify has a range of MagSafe leather wallets in artist-made designs.

BEST stick-on WALLET

Elusefor Stick-On Phone Wallet

For a true all-in-one solution, Elusefor’s stick-on phone wallet features a built-in elastic band for added security, plus a handy key ring attachment. Simply stick the wallet onto your phone or case using the supplied 3M adhesive. Compatible with iPhone and Android and available in a variety of colors.

BEST MULTI-CARD HOLDER

StickyWallet Spandex Stick-on Phone Wallet

If you can’t leave home without multiple cards and cash, consider StickyWallet’s spandex phone wallet, featuring a stretchy double pocket design to carry up to five cards and up to ten bills. This wallet sticks to your phone with that trusty 3M advesive tape, and offers unique design touches like a secret magnetic plate so you can attach to a magnetic phone mount. Compatible with most smartphones.

BEST MAGSAFE WALLET

ESR Magnetic Wallet

BEST CONCEALED DESIGN

The wallet’s extra strong magnets promise to stay put, and the smooth vegan leather gives it a high-end feel.
