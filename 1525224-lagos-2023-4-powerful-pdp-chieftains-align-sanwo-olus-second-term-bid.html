Lagos 2023: 4 Powerful PDP Chieftains Align, Back Sanwo-Olus Second-Term Bid

  • Good news beckons for Governor Babajide Sanwo-Olu as some top members of the opposition political parties back his second-term bid
  • Reports making the rounds reveal that Peoples Democratic Party and Labour Party chieftains have pledged their support for the Lagos state governor
  • The strong opposition members include Deji Doherty, Dr Ade Dosunmu, Goke Salvador and Rahman Owokoniran

The second-term bid of Governor Babajide Sanwo-Olu has received a massive boost following an endorsement from four powerful chieftains of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP).

As reported by The Nation, these PDP chieftains include Deji Doherty, Dr Ade Dosunmu, Goke Salvador and Rahman Owokoniran.

The quartet vowed to drum support for the Governor Sanwo-Olu of the All Progressive Congress (APC) and stage a mobilisation campaign to support his second-term agenda.
