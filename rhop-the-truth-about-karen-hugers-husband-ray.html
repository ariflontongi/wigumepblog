The Truth About Karen Huger's Husband, Ray

By Season 5, fans watched as Karen and Ray Huger struggled to bring back the intimacy in their relationship. While Karen worked to serenade and romance her husband after becoming the breadwinner in the marriage, Ray found it difficult to tell her how much he loved her. According to what the businessman said in their couple's therapy session, he wanted more attention and for her to cook more. He also said "I think I am" when asked if he was in love with his wife. Ouch!

When discussing the change in their onscreen dynamic in Season 5 in comparison to earlier seasons, Karen said the couple was "raw this season" while speaking with Bravo's Daily Dish. She even shared that things got so tense between them, she "didn't know where we were gonna go." 

No matter what, they've remained a team with a solid union. In September 2020, they celebrated their 24th wedding anniversary. "Happy anniversary to us," she captioned a photo of the two. "24 years and counting," she added. The Grand Dame and Black Bill Gates are still going strong.
