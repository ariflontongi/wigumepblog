Dermstore has sales throughout the year on skincare and makeup essentials. You can save up to 30% off in the Dermstore sale with popular brands, such as SkinCeuticals sales. Dermstore also has sales on key dates such as Black Friday and Cyber Monday, so check back here for sales updates.

Is there a Dermstore loyalty program?

Yes. When you sign up for the Dermstore Rewards loyalty program, you can earn points when you shop. For every purchase from the online store, you can earn up to 5 points to use in the future. You can also earn 5% cashback on each order and be sent early sales. 

What is the return policy at Dermstore?

You can return Dermstore products within 60 days of the purchase date. Your order should be unopened, unused, and with any cosmetic or fragrance seals still intact. Returns can be made via the Dermstore Returns Policy page, and the returns courier ZigZag.
