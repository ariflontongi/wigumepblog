&quot;You Can Save N895k Monthly&quot;: Man Counters Air Peace CEO, Explains Why Earning 2000 in UK is Bette

  • A Nigerian man took time to explain why it is better to earn £2000 (over N3.5 million) in the UK than earn N200,000 in Nigeria
  • The man, Tosin Olugbenga, noted that anyone who lived in the UK and earned £2000 could conveniently save up £500 (over N895k) monthly
  • He said after 10 years of savings, someone earning £2000 in the UK could move to Nigeria and invest, unlike someone who earned N200k for that period of time

A Nigerian man has said the claim that someone earning N200,000 in Nigeria is better than someone earning £2000 in the UK, does not hold water.

Tosin Olugbenga took time to counter the statement attributed to Air Peace CEO, Allen Onyema, who said someone earning £2000 in the UK was not financially well-off.
