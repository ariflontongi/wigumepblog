Toni Braxton Clears The Air Around Her And Birdman's Rumored Engagement: &quot;We Are Both Single&quot;

Toni Braxton has finally decided to speak out after rumors circled her and Birdman this past weekend. The singer and co-founder of Cash Money Records have been linked for years and even split about four years ago to the day. However, things began to ramp up around them after word got out that the two got married quite privately. Toni's sister Tamar Braxton went on Instagram Live to address all of it.  "I've been blowing up her phone," she said

She might have felt left out in that moment but was nothing but happy for the singing icon. But, it seems it was all a hoax. According to XXL, Toni shut all of that down, replying to an Instagram post on their supposed wedding. She wrote back, saying, "My dear friend @birdman and I are not married...never been married. We are both single."

Toni Braxton Stops The Confusion

Now it seems the on-again-off-again couple is now split once more. They seemed to be doing well about a month and a half ago, however. Toni and Birdman shared a photo of each other getting snuggly together, with the caption, "Sending Sunday kisses. @birdman." More might come on that bit of information. But, for now, we know the marriage was never a thing.
