Blake Lively reveals she worked out 13 hours a day for her new film

    Blake Lively has revealed that she took her fitness routine to a whole new level while filming her latest film . The 28-year-old actress admitted that she underwent a gruelling routine to get in shape for the action-packed thriller, which she began filming a few months after she and husband Ryan Reynolds welcomed their baby daughter James.

    Blake Lively has spoken about her gruelling routine during 

    "I was working out 13 hours a day because shooting was working out," the former star told . "It was neat to be able to do that, and it was neat to have that challenge after having a baby, because you think your body is so different – you think no matter what, it's never totally [going to] be the same."

    In the film, Blake stars as a student who becomes stranded on a giant rock, 200 yards from shore, with a great white shark lurking in the water ready to attack. Therefore, it comes as no surprise that her training involved a lot of swimming, which the star credited for helping her to get "stronger and more fit".

    The actress did a lot of swimming while filming the action-packed thriller

    "Being in waves like that, swimming like that, doing such long takes like that all the time, I became so much stronger and more fit by the end of production," she explained, crediting her "amazing trainer" Don Saladino for helping her to get in shape "in the most healthy way".

    Blake also revealed that husband Ryan encouraged her to take on the challenge. "He was very kind and generous to read the script," she said. "I was really sick and I was working, and so he read it and he was like, 'Aww, the action is amazing.'"

    Blake and Ryan are expecting their second child together

    However, the blonde beauty confessed that Ryan warned her that it would be a demanding role to take on, telling her: "Be prepared, this movie is a physical challenge, it is an athletic event. It was like training for a marathon."

    As she promotes the thriller which is out later this month, Blake will no doubt also be making the most of her downtime, following the news that she and Ryan are expecting their second child together.

    The couple are said to be over the moon as they prepare to welcome the new arrival, as a source told "Blake is absolutely thrilled and so is Ryan."
