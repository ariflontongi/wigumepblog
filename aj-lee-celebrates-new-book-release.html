AJ Lee Celebrates New Book Release

AJ Lee left WWE and the pro wrestling world behind her. She has been very busy writing in the meantime, and today is a special day for her career after pro wrestling.

The new Dungeons & Dragons: At the Spine of the World book is out now, and fans are certain to recognize one name associated with the book. AJ Lee was happy to tweet out a couple photos of her posing with the new comic book that she helped pen.

Our new comic book Dungeons & Dragons: At the Spine of the World is on shelves now! And if the shelves look like this, it might be because I attacked your shop.

AJ Lee hasn’t shown any interest in making a return to pro wrestling. CM Punk has teased the idea of making a return just for the sake of having some fun with the idea, but nothing has come of it. Fans can pick up a copy of AJ Lee’s new comic book today.
