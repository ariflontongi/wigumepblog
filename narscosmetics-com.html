FAQs

How can I get free shipping at NARS?

NARS has free standard shipping for all orders with FedEx this holiday season. If you are signed up to ShopRunners, you can also qualify for free shipping on orders of $25 or more. Be sure to check this page for the occasional NARS free shipping promo code.  

How can I get a NARS promo code on first orders?

Sign up for the NARS newsletter on their website to get 15% off your first order. You’ll also be kept updated on the latest NARS promotions, new cosmetics, and occasional NARS promo codes.  

Does NARS cosmetics go on sale?

NARS does have sales throughout the year. One of the best ways to shop these sales is by visiting the NARS homepage. NARS also has sales on key shopping dates, such as Black Friday or New Year's. As savings experts, we’ve previously noted up to 40% off during their sales on selected products such as lipsticks.  

How can I get free samples at NARS?

Like many beauty brands, NARS offers free samples of beauty and skincare products with its orders. You might need to spend a specific amount to qualify for this. Our savings experts have previously noted free gift promotions as well, including a free mini blush when you spend $50. 

What is the returns policy at NARS?

Returns can be made for free to NARS within 30 days of purchasing. Your items will need to be returned as new or in ‘gently used’ condition. If you are returning a makeup kit, all items must be returned for a complete refund.
