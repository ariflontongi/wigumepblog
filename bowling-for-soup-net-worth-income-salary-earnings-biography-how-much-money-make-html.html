In 1994, the Wichita Falls, Texas-based rock band Bowling for Soup (commonly abbreviated as BFS) came together to create their first album, Bowling for Soup! (Bowling for Soup!). The band, which is currently headquartered in Denton, Texas, is best known for its songs “Girl All the Bad Guys Want” (which was nominated for a Grammy Award in 2003), “Almost,” “Punk Rock 101,” “High School Never Ends,” and the SR-71 tribute “1985”. Earlier this year, on April 8, 2013, Bowling for Soup announced that their last tour of the United Kingdom will take place in October that year.
