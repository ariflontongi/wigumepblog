Brendan Gleeson Bio, Wife, Family, Kids, Net worth, Height

Brendan Gleeson worked as a teacher for ten years before venturing into the acting world. He got his big break after taking a place as one of the most recognizable characters in the Harry Potter series as Alastor Moody. Since then he has become one of the most recognizable faces in the world and has starred in other projects such as In Bruges, Edge of Tomorrow, Paddington 2. He is a Golden Globe nominee and an Emmy winner. Find out more about Brendan Gleeson by reading below.

Brendan Gleeson’s bio

Sometimes the biggest obstacle in our journey to success is us. Brendan Gleeson worked as a teacher for ten years before deciding to pursue his dream of becoming an actor. Today, hesitation has only cost him ten more years that he could have had as a professional actor.

His long road to success began from secondary school, a few years after his birth on March 29, 1955 in Dublin, Ireland. He was born to parents Frank Gleeson and Pat Gleeson. Brendan Gleeson later fell in love with acting, a profession that required reading many screenplays. So it was good that he fell so much in love with reading at a young age that he is described as an avid reader to himself and to those who knew him at the time. For his education he was tutored at St. Joseph’s CBS in Dublin. It was at St. Joseph’s that he fell in love with acting after serving in the school’s drama group.

Despite his great passion for acting, Brendan tried to play it safe and became a teacher after completing his acting training and teaching secondary school students at Catholic Belcamp College in North County Dublin. He taught Irish and English. Luckily, our future actor Alastor Moody felt good about not giving up his dream entirely and worked on various stage productions for the theater company Passion Machine.

During this time he appeared in notable plays such as Brownbread, Home and Wasters. As a later director at Passion Machine, he provided a glimpse into its history by writing a number of plays for Passion Machine, including Breaking Up and The Birdtable.

After 10 years of combining the acting few responsibilities that came with his responsibilities as a school teacher, he retired from teaching and became a full-time actor in 1991. His first onscreen project was in a TV movie in 1989. Dear Sarah as Brendan Dowd. The following year he made his big screen debut in Das Feld , where he played the character Steinhauer .

Since debuting in both media, Brendan has gone on to act in multiple films and TV shows, racking up credits and establishing himself as a great actor, both in his Irish/English film industry and in Hollywood. In film, he has appeared in a total of 77 films including three of the films in the Harry Potter series; Gangs of New York, Troy, Bruges, Heart of the Sea among others. He acted on television In den Sturm, den contract, that’s my father, among other things, with a total of 14 credits, which are mostly television films. He has quite a few awards and nominations to his name such as an Emmy award, two BIFA awards, three Golden Globe nominations and two BAFTA nominations.

Wealth (income and earnings)

Whatever they pay teachers, it sure can’t be compared to an actor’s financial earnings, especially when he’s as successful as Brendan. He has been able to earn a net worth of $4 million through his appearances in multiple films spanning over 80 films and TV shows.

Family – wife and children

Brendan Gleeson, despite his fame and fortune, has quite simply nurtured life. He has been married to one woman, Mary Weldon, since 1982. The marriage produced four children, two of whom followed in their father’s footsteps and were actors – Domhnall and Brian Gleeson. Domhnall has featured in several films such as Ex Machina, The Revenant and About Time . His other children are – Fergus Gleeson and Rory Gleeson.

Height

Brendan’s height, which is not a typical Hollywood performer’s physique, was one of his tools to play famous roles in movies. He stands at a height of 6 feet 2 inches with a body weight of 90 kg. He is a man with blond hair and blue eyes.
