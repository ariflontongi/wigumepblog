2

Halle Berry: "Ginger Knowles"

Media Platforms Design Team

- 2 oz. Pierde Almas Mezcal
- .75 oz. Aperol
- .75 oz. Ginger Pineapple Syrup
- .5 oz. Fresh Lime Juice

Add all ingredients in an ice filled mixing glass. Shake, strain over fresh ice into a pilsner glass. Garnish with a piece of candied ginger.

Inspiration: Named after Halle Berry's character in Swordfish, this one is sweet, spicy, and smokey, all in one, just like the lady herself.

3

Jennifer Aniston: "Old Friends"

Media Platforms Design Team

- 1 oz. Rittenhouse Rye Whiskey
- 1 oz. Cynar
- 1 oz. Noilly Prat Dry Vermouth

Combine ingredients in an ice filled mixing glass and stir vigorously for 25 seconds. Garnish with a lemon peel.

Inspiration: This is a variation of a classic cocktail called the Old Pal, which is in turn, a variation on the Negroni. It will be there for you.

4

Jennifer Lawrence: "The Girl On Fire"

Media Platforms Design Team

- 2oz Jalapeno Infused El Jimador Tequila
- 1.5oz Fresh Squeezed Grapefruit Juice
- .75oz Orange Curacao
- .50oz Fresh Squeezed Lime Juice

Shake all in all the ingredients and strain over fresh ice into a pilsner glass with a chipotle salted rim. Garnish with a lime wedge.

Inspiration: Katniss feels the heat constantly in The Hunger Games, and so will you with this spicy sipper.

5

Leonardo DiCaprio: "Blood Diamond"

Media Platforms Design Team

- 1.5 oz. Beefeater Gin
- 3 oz. "Ghost Mix"

Shake ingredients and strain over ice into a rocks glass. Garnish with a cucumber wheel. To make a "ghost mix" you start by making a high quality Bloody Mary mix, let it sit overnight, then strain out the solids using cheesecloth. You're left with a mixture that has all that Bloody Mary flavor, but a much lighter consistency.

Inspiration: I imagine this as the kind of drink Danny Archer's (Dicaprio's fictional character) relatives might enjoy.

7

Jared Leto: "Paul Allen's Demise"

Media Platforms Design Team

- 4 oz. Absolut Elyx Vodka
- .25 oz Noilly Prat Extra Dry Vermouth

Combine ingredients in an ice filled mixing glass. Stir vigorously for 40-50 rotations. Strain into a chilled large coupe and garnish with a jalapeño stuffed olive.

Inspiration: A double Absolut Martini is the drink that Jared Leto's short lived, but memorable, character orders during his fateful dinner with Patrick Bateman. It packs a wallop and is best enjoyed while listening to Huey Lewis and the News.

8

Anne Hathaway: "The White Queen"

Media Platforms Design Team

- 2 oz. Machu Pisco
- .5 oz. Orgeat Syrup
- .5 oz. Fresh Lime Juice

Add all ingredients in a pint, top with ice. Shake well and strain into a coupe. Garnish: 3 drops hibiscus syrup.

Inspiration: This is a take on a Pisco Sour, but without the egg white. It's pure as the driven snow with a kiss of red. Not unlike Anne Hathaway's character in Alice in Wonderland.

9

Johnny Depp: "Sleepy Hollow"

Media Platforms Design Team

- 1.75 oz Noah's Mill Bourbon
- .5 oz. Spiced Apple Syrup
- .5 oz. Fresh Lemon Juice
- .25 oz. Averna

Add all ingredients in a pint, top with ice. Shake, strain over fresh ice into a rocks glass. Garnish with a flamed orange peel.

Inspiration: To make a spiced apple syrup, make a hot cider, spiced to taste with cinnamon, clove, and allspice. Then , dissolve into it an equal amount of sugar. This cocktail is perfect for a quiet fall night. Just don't drink too many and lose your head.

Ryan Gosling: "The Driver"
