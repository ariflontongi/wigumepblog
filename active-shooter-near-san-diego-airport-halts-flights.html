Active Shooter Near San Diego Airport Halts Flights

Flights are currently being barred from landing at the San Diego International Airport due to an active shooter at an apartment complex in the area. Departures are being allowed despite the complex being in the area of approaching planes. Officers say it is a domestic violence situation and gunshots have been going off sporadically since this morning.
