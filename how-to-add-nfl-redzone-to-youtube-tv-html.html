Calling All Couch Potato Touchdown Enthusiasts: How to Snag NFL RedZone on YouTube TV

Ah, the glorious fall season. Leaves are changing, pumpkin spice lattes are flowing freely, and Sundays are dedicated to the one true religion: football. But with so many games happening at once, how do you, the discerning fan, avoid the dreaded "channel-flipping fatigue"? Fear not, my friend, for there's a magical solution called NFL RedZone, and it's here to transform your Sundays from FOMO-filled to touchdown-thrilling.

But wait, how do you get this magical beast onto your YouTube TV viewing experience? Buckle up, because I'm about to break it down for you easier than a wide receiver burning past a linebacker (although, that might not be that easy...).

There Be Two Ways to Get There, Folks: The Sports Plus Pass or the Sunday Ticket Shuffle

There are two main paths to NFL RedZone nirvana on YouTube TV. Let's explore both, so you can choose your champion:

1. The Sports Plus Pass: A Buffet of Sports Goodness

For a monthly fee, you can snag the Sports Plus Pass. This bad boy is like a sports channel buffet, offering NFL RedZone alongside a smorgasbord of other sports networks. It's perfect if you're a well-rounded sports enthusiast who enjoys diving into more than just football (though, we all know football reigns supreme).

2. The Sunday Ticket Shuffle (For the Truly Dedicated)

This option involves NFL Sunday Ticket, which lets you watch any NFL game live, out-of-market. Now, here's the twist: sometimes, NFL Sunday Ticket throws in NFL RedZone as a bonus. But be warned, it's not always guaranteed, so check before you commit.

Important Note: This option might require some additional fees, so be sure to do your research before diving in.

So, You've Chosen Your Path. Now What?

Once you've decided between the buffet or the gamble, adding NFL RedZone is a breeze. Here's the skinny:

  • Head over to your YouTube TV settings.
  • Navigate to the "Add-ons" section.
  • Find your chosen champion (Sports Plus Pass or NFL Sunday Ticket, if it includes RedZone).
  • Click "Add" and follow the on-screen instructions.

    • Voila! You're now ready for a season filled with nonstop football highlights, courtesy of NFL RedZone.

    Pro Tip: Don't forget to set those DVR recordings for the full games you want to watch later. NFL RedZone is great for catching the action, but sometimes, you just gotta see the whole story unfold.

    Frequently Asked Questions: Your NFL RedZone on YouTube TV Conundrums Solved

    1. How much does the Sports Plus Pass cost?

    The price can vary slightly, but expect to pay around $10.99 per month.

    2. Does the Sunday Ticket Shuffle always include NFL RedZone?

    Nope, not guaranteed. Double-check before you subscribe to see if it's included that season.

    3. Can I watch NFL RedZone on my phone or tablet?

    Absolutely! As long as you have a YouTube TV subscription and the relevant add-on, you can catch the action anywhere with an internet connection.

    4. Is there a free trial for the Sports Plus Pass?

    YouTube TV sometimes offers free trials, so keep an eye out for those!

    5. Can I watch NFL RedZone without YouTube TV?

    Yes, there are other streaming services that offer NFL RedZone. However, YouTube TV is a great option because it combines live TV channels with the ability to add NFL RedZone.
