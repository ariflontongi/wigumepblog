Oba Saheed Ademola Elegushi: 6 Times Popular Yoruba King Gave Royal Fashionista Goals in Cute Pictur

One of the youngest Nigerian kings in Nigeria is Oba Saheed Ademola Elegushi, the Elegushi of Ikate Kingdom. The traditional ruler is known for his great dress sense and Legit.ng takes a look into his fashion life.

Legit.ng presents 6 times Oba Saheed Ademola Elegushi gave royal fashionista goals in cute pictures

1. Stepping out in Agbada and three quarter trouser

Oba Elegushi obviously loves white traditional attires. He is seen stepping out in his white Agbada and three quarter trouser. A purple cap sits atop his head as royal beads grace his neck, wrists and ankles.

2. Decked in white polo and face cap

The king is seen in another picture putting on white attire. But this time around he is wearing a polo and a face cap. If he is to be described in simple words, one will say Oba Elegushi is cool, calm and collected.

This picture has further proven that white is the best colours of the traditional ruler.

3. Decked in a blue traditional attire

The monarch knows how to bring out the beauty of the Yoruba traditional attire, and that is what he has done in the photo below.

He is decked in a blue traditional attire with marching cap. Royalty is written all over him in the adorable photo.

4. He is a lover of face cap

In the photo below, he is seeing wearing a blue face cap with a designer shirt and a blue trouser. The traditional ruler complements the dressing with nice sneakers and a beautiful smile.

5. Polo and cap all the way

The traditional ruler is seen putting on a polo, cap and a dark shade. The polo is inscribed with the words Royalty Forever.

In the pose Oba Elegushi strikes in the picture, he seems to be staring into the future and seeing the many blessings on the way.
