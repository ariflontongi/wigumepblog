JiDion gets himself banned from all NBA and WNBA events after new prank

YouTube pranksterJiDion won't be welcomed at any NBA-related events (including the WNBA) after getting banned by the league.

JiDion posted a new video on his account, in which he attempted to pull off one of his famous pranks of doing unusual activities at major sporting events.

JiDion's WNBA jump shot

In a recent WNBA game between the Minnesota Lynks and the Dallas Wings, JiDion was attempting to sleep on courtside seats. Before he could complete the prank he found himself deflecting a ball that went flying towards the crowd.

JiDion then took the ball and attempted a jump shot from the sideline. The ball went nowhere near the basket but the antic left players perplexed.

Shortly after a security usher escorted him out and told him he could be served with a trespass.

Because he couldn't attempt the sleep prank, JiDeon then made his way to a Sparks game to try his luck in Los Angeles.

JiDion's sleep apnea prank

After securing a few courtside seats at a Sparks game, JiDion is seen dressed in the same pajama set as before, and carrying a pillow and a blanket, attempting to fall asleep in the WNBA game because "his doctor recommended it".

A staff member from the venue arrived shortly after he started the prank to give him the warning.

JiDion kept going and even managed to lay down completely flat in the chairs before the staff member returned with security reinforcements who ended up escorting him out of the arena.

Fans of the YouTube star didn't stay quiet after the ban was made public.

Hundreds took to social media to troll the WNBA's accounts by posting comments of the sleeping smiley face emojis and GIF's of sleeping characters demanding that the league "free JiDion".
