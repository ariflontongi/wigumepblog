Logic Bounces Back With 19-Year-Old Blonde Following Divorce

Not even a full month following the public announcement of his divorce to Jessica Andrea, Logic looks to be rebounding with a new girl. Three weeks ago, Logic confirmed the rumors swirling around that he and his wife Jessica would be splitting up in a public statement where he wrote that things "just didn't work out." Noting that there was no anger, cheating or any bad blood between the two, it seemed as though Bobby just wanted to be single. Well, it looks like the rapper had enough of life by himself and is cuddling up to a brand new girl.

TMZ spotted the "Everyday" rapper boarding a private jet with a new girl in tow as his divorce looms. The 19-year-old is apparently named Brittney and the two met at a juice shop in Calabasas that Logic regularly visits. According to TMZ, Brittney is an employee at the shop. While there are no clear shots of the girl's face in the video, the two walk up the plane's steps one after the other, and signs are pointing to Logic not being as single as he claimed to be.
