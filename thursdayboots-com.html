FAQs

Does Thursday Boots have free shipping?

Thursday Boots does have free standard shipping on all orders of $50 or above. If your order is less than this amount, standard shipping will be calculated at the checkout. It’s good to know return shipping is also free on orders over $80.  

Does Thursday Boots have sales?

The best time to save on your Thursday Boots purchase is usually during their annual Black Friday sale and Warehouse Sale. You can make sure you catch these sales by signing up for the Thursday Boots newsletter. This is also a great way to know what kind of savings you could grab!

How do I get a 10% email discount at Thursday Boots?

We sometimes have a 10% off discount code for Thursday Boots. Check this page regularly to see if we have this Thursday Boots promo code. You might also get early sales and exclusive discounts by signing up for the Thursday Boots email newsletter. 

Does Thursday Boots have a military discount?

Unfortunately, Thursday Boots does not offer a military discount code at the time of writing. However, to support the Armed Forces, they will cover the shipping fee of USPS Priority shipping to any military residential address. 

Can I get a Thursday Boots gift card?

Yes, Thursday Boots offers digital gift cards with values ranging from $30 to $500. To purchase a Thursday Boot gift card, open the drop-down for either men or women, click the 'gift card' to select your amount and add your digital Thursday gift card to your cart.

What is the return policy at Thursday Boots?

You can return your Thursday Boots order within 30 days. If your order was $80 or above, you can return your order for free. Your shoes or items should be returned unworn, undamaged, and with their original packaging. Thursday Boots specify that shoes should be in resellable condition with no creases on the soles. You can use the Return Portal on their website to return your shoes.

Save with Sales: Start shopping incredibly rare promotions at Thursday Boots such as a free gift on each purchase for Black Friday weekend. Thursday Boots also has a warehouse sale each year, where you can find all their signature boots like the Captain, Legend, and President at up to 50% off.

You’ll also receive email alerts for new product launches, the rare sale, and the elusive Thursday Boots discounts to use on your next purchase. Joining is easy, just put your email address in the box at the bottom of the Thursday Boot webpage. 

Buy Pre-loved: If you’re on a tight budget emailing Thursday Boot directly could be your best option. They sometimes offer lightly used or factory seconds for a heavily discounted price. However, this one is a bit of luck of the draw because they don't list what shoes they have available, you simply have to email your interest by listing the design and size you’re after and hope they get back to you.
