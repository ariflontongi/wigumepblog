FAQs

Does Drunk Elephant have free shipping?

Yes, Drunk Elephant offers free ground shipping on orders over $40. You can also get free two-day shipping with any order over $200. With a Shoprunner account, you can get free two-day shipping on orders over $25. 

What are the delivery options at Drunk Elephant?

Aside from the shipping allowances listed above, ground shipping will cost $6.95. This will be delivered within 5-7 working days. Two-day shipping will cost $14.95 and be delivered in 2 working days. You can also choose delivery to US military addresses, Hawaii, and Alaska for extra costs.  

Are Drunk Elephant cruelty-free?

Yes, Drunk Elephant states on its website it is 100% cruelty-free and certified by the Leaping Bunny Program. It is also listed by PETA as a cruelty-free brand. They have never tested on animals or asked others to do so.  

When are the Drunk Elephant sales?

Whilst Drunk Elephant does have sales, these only happen from now and then. One of the main sales at Drunk Elephant is its Black Friday sale, with previous discounts of 20% off sitewide. 

What is the return policy at Drunk Elephant?

You can make a return to Drunk Elephant within 30 days. You will need to contact the customer service team to start your return. Returns can be made with ‘gently used’ products and Drunk Elephant will give a complete refund, minus shipping costs.
