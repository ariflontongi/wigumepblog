Jordan Patrick Smith's early life

The actor was born on June 18, 1989, in Fife, United Kingdom. Growing up in Scotland, Smith was a talented footballer and played for Dunfermline's youth academy.

He studied at St Columba's High School in Scotland. Following a holiday to Australia in 2001, Smith's family fell in love with the country and decided to emigrate. They moved to Brisbane in 2003 when Smith was 13 years old. Smith later attended Carmel Catholic College in Australia.
